waybar

Add these two sections to enable a taskbar through the toplevel-foreign protocol:

"modules-left": ["wlr/taskbar"],
    "wlr/taskbar": {
        "format": "{app_id}",
       "on-click": "minimize",
       "on-click-right": "maximize",
    }
